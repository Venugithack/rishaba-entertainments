Who We Are

A modern Tamil entertainment house

Rishaba Entertainments (M/S Rishaba Entertainments) is a Chennai-based film production and content company. We produce Tamil feature films and develop premium OTT web series rooted in strong writing, cultural depth and pan-Indian appeal.

Led by entrepreneur, retail investor and film producer S. Durga Janani, the company combines commercial scalability with creative execution — managing projects end to end, from script identification and production management through to distribution alignment and OTT partnerships.

“To build scalable businesses and impactful cinematic content by bridging commerce, creativity and audience engagement.” — Our Vision

Web Series & OTT

In Development Co-Production Premium OTT

7 Kottai Veedu

A premium OTT web series adaptation of the work by acclaimed Tamil & Malayalam author Jeyamohan. Planned with Sasikumar and Manju Warrier in pivotal roles, directed by Mandhira Moorthy (Ayothi) and Virumandi (Ka / Pae Ranasingam). Developed as a high-value co-production for a leading OTT platform.

Role — Co-Producer

In Development In-House Original

Role — Producer (In-House Original)
