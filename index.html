Who We Are

A modern Tamil entertainment house

Rishaba Entertainments is a Chennai-based powerhouse for high-concept Tamil cinema and premium streaming originals. We build intellectual property that travels — across platforms, across languages, across audiences that do not think in borders. Every project is nurtured from script to screen through a meticulously integrated process spanning creative development, production, theatrical distribution and premium OTT partnerships — each decision guided by cultural authenticity, cinematic craftsmanship and long-term commercial value.

At the centre of this operation is S. Durga Janani, a dynamic entrepreneur, retail investor and producer whose background brings rare financial discipline to a volatile industry. Under her leadership, the studio acts as a sophisticated incubator for intellectual property, bridging creative excellence with marketplace scale.

“To build scalable businesses and impactful cinematic content by bridging commerce, creativity and audience engagement.” — Our Vision
